GENARAL PROBLEMS

1. What is PIXY ?
PIXY is a telephone application with the easiest way to broadcast live video; you can share what’s happening around you with the whole world, whenever and wherever.
2. What is broadcast/live ?
Broadcast/live is a new way to share and communicate, the produce of the video and interaction with audience can be done at the same time.
3. What is diamond ?
Diamond is the negotiable virtual currency you can buy in PIXY; you can use it to support your favorite broadcaster.
4. What is Star ?
As a broadcaster, if your fans send you gift to show their support, the gift will be converting to certain stars; with enough stars, you can exchange those stars for diamond or cash.

RECHARGE PROBLEMS

1. How to buy Diamond ?
In PIXY>Personal homepage>Diamonds, you can buy Diamond according to your needs.
2. How to pay for Diamonds ?
For iOS user, you can buy with Appstore in-app purchase; for Android user, you can buy with Googleplay in-app purchase.
3. If I bought diamond in a wrong account, can I get my money back ?
Sorry, once you finished your payment, you cannot recall it, please check your bill carefully before you confirm; we will also do our best to remind you of every detail.

4. Is there a limit of how many diamonds I can recharge?
There is no limit of diamonds you can buy, you can recharge for as much as you want.
5. What if the recharge failed?
First of all, please check your bank card balance. If the payment has not made, you can try again, if the recharge still fail, please reinstall PIXY and log in with the same account, then try recharge. If the payment has made, please contact the online customer service. There will be a special-assigned support staff to help you.
6. I have paid the bill, but the diamonds in Balance remains unchanged?
Please check your bank card balance and make sure the payment has been made. Then contact the online customer service, and provide the material listed below so we can solve the problem faster.
1). Your PIXY ID
2). The specific time you made the deal.
3). A screenshot of the bill.

LIVE PROBLEMS

1. How to broadcast ?
Tap the Camera-shaped icon in the middle of home page to start broadcasting, before the broadcast officially begin, you can set title for the broadcast or share the broadcast, and so on.
2. I got a notification that said PIXY has no access to camera and microphone, what should I do?
For iOS user, go to settings>Privacy, find Camera and Microphone, and enable each access for PIXY. For Android user, there is similar setting in the system Settings.
3. Why the image of video is sometimes reversed ?
For some broadcasting using the front camera, the image is captured and transmitted this way, it will not affect normal use.
MORE
4. what if there is no one watching my broadcast?
First, you can share your broadcast to other social networks , so you can access more people; then be humorous and positive, and interact with audiences as much as you can, the more you react to your audiences, the longer will they stay.
5. What is Bullets?
A bullet message is one kind of message that drifts and passes the middle of the screen, it's the best way to distinguish your message from others and gain the broadcasters attention.
6. I did not receive a notification for the beginning of the broadcasters I am following.
If the broadcaster made a broadcast and you did not receive notification, please check your PIXY notification settings for this broadcaster and system notification settings for PIXY.
7. What should I do if someone sent insulting or harassing message?
If you are making the broadcast, you can mute this person, just type the message this person sent, select manage and select mute, the person will not be able to message you. You can also set someone you know well as your administrator in broadcasting to help you block such person.If you are watching a broadcast, and someone is sending inappropriate messages, you can choose to report this person the same way. But we do not encourage malicious report.
8. How to follow and unfollow someone?
Go to this person home page, or tap someone’s message in a broadcasting to view his/her information, tap the icon representing follow you will follow this person, take it slow, one more tap you will unfollow this person.
9. How to make the broadcast a hot one?
All the hot broadcasts are recommended by the system randomly, but there will be greater chance to become hot if you broadcast as many as you can, and you have many audiences watching your broadcast.
10. When watching a broadcast, how to get a clear view of the view without so many messages?
Slide the screen left, you will get a clear view without messages or bullets; slide right will bring things back.
11. How to share the broadcast?
As a broadcaster, you can choose to share your broadcast before you start or while you are broadcasting, before the broadcast, you can select one way to share; While you are broadcasting, you can tap the icon representing share to share to different apps each time. If you are watch someone else’s broadcast, you can do this as well.
12. Why my messages were delayed sometimes?
When there are too many messages at one time, to make sure every message shown clearly, some of the message will be delayed, it’s inevitable, you can choose to send bullets if you don’t want the broadcaster miss you message.

ACCOUNT PROBLEMS

1. I can’t log in.
If you have trouble logging into PIXY app: Make sure you have the latest version of the PIXY app. Shut down app, and then reopen to try to log in again. Forgot your password? Try to reset the password.
2. How to edit my profile ?
Tap the icon on the right top of screen in Profile page and then you can edit your personal profile. One special note: Gender can be set only once.
3. I find PIXY takes up too much memory after I watch live for some time.
Access Profile/Settings and choose Manage Storage, the cache content will be cleared. This will not affect you to use this app.
MORE
4. I received a warning. What to do?
Receiving a warning means some of your action violated the PIXY live policy. If you continue to violate, PIXY will handle your account according to the policy, including but not limited to freezing your account temporarily, or permanently. In order to build PIXY to be a healthy, friendly live platform, please follow the policy strictly.
5. Why did my live broadcast stop suddenly?
According to the PIXY live policy, if your action was found to have serious violation, PIXY reserve the right to force stop your live broadcast and report to the related department for further punishment.
6. Why did I receive a message that my account is frozen?
According to the PIXY live policy, if your action was found to have serious violation, PIXY may freeze your account temporarily or even permanently. In order to build PIXY to be a healthy, friendly live platform, please follow the policy strictly. If you don’t accept the reason for account freezing, please contact customer service in Profile/Settings/Users Feedback.
7. My account is frozen. How to appeal?
If you don’t accept the reason for account freezing, you can contact customer service in Profile/Settings/Users Feedback and appeal.
8. When can I appeal?
You can appeal within 10 days after account was frozen. The appeal application will not be accepted after 10 days.
